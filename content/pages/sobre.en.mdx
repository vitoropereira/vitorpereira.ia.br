I started my career on an air traffic control radar. Years making decisions
under pressure, with zero margin for error — where "roughly right" doesn't
exist. That discipline of critical operations is what I brought to software.
And today, to AI.

I'm **Vitor Pereira**. I work with **applied AI in business**: the part that
runs in production and delivers results — not the pretty demo. I build and
operate autonomous agents, in-product AI copilots, automation pipelines, and
data systems that solve real problems, end to end.

I use AI as an operating system to deliver the output of an entire team — solo,
with real numbers behind it.
